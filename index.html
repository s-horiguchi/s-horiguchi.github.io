Shuhei A. Horiguchi

Kanazawa, Japan · shuhe-remove-i.a.horiguchi@gmail.com

Shuhei A. Horiguchi (堀口 修平) is an assistant professor at Nano Life Science Institute (Nano-LSI), Kanazawa University in OKUDA LAB led by Satoru Okuda. I have obtained PhD in March 2024 at the University of Tokyo under the supervision of Tetsuya J. Kobayashi.

I am interested in an analogy between artificial and biological systems.

Research interest:

  • Information processing in the multicellular system (e.g., immune system, nervous system, embryogenesis)
  • Robotics / Computer science
